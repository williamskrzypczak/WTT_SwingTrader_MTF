üéØ Core Concept

The WTT Swing Trader MTF combines multi-timeframe trend analysis with current timeframe signal generation to identify high-probability Syzygy trading opportunities. The indicator requires alignment across Daily, 4-hour, 1-hour, and current timeframes for maximum signal reliability.

üìä Multi-Timeframe Analysis

Daily Timeframe (Primary)

Establishes the overall market trend using EMA alignment (12, 26, 50 periods). All signals require daily trend confirmation.

4-Hour Timeframe

Provides intermediate trend confirmation. Must align with daily trend for signal validation.

1-Hour Timeframe

Offers short-term trend confirmation. Must align with higher timeframes for signal validation.

Current Timeframe

Generates entry signals based on local momentum and volume confirmation.

üéØ Trading Strategy

Conservative Approach (Default)

The indicator uses conservative settings by default, requiring strong multi-timeframe alignment for signal generation. This reduces false signals and focuses on high-probability trading opportunities.

Entry Criteria

  • Multi-timeframe trend alignment
  • MACD momentum confirmation
  • RSI in optimal ranges
  • Above-average volume
  • Price relative to fast EMA

Risk Management

  • Use boundary lines as support/resistance
  • Monitor trend cloud breaches
  • Watch for ATR exit signals
  • Follow multi-timeframe trend direction
